🛡️ Quick Safety Tips

  • Always stay aware of your surroundings.
  • Keep emergency numbers on speed dial.
  • Let someone know your location when going out late.
  • Use loud or bright signals to attract attention if needed.

👮‍♂️ Authorities to Contact

Use 112 for any urgent help in most regions.

If abroad, check local emergency numbers in advance.
